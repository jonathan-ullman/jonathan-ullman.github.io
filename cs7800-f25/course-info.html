CS 7800: Advanced Algorithms

Fall 2025

Overview

This is an advanced course in algorithms aimed at PhD students in computer science and related fields. The course surveys fundamental topics like optimization—greedy algorithms, dynamic programming, network flow, and linear and convex programming—randomized algorithms, online algorithms, NP-completeness. The course emphasizes how to reason and communicate rigorously about algorithms.
