Interested in working with me as an undergraduate? That's great, I love working with undergraduates when possible. My suggestion is to first contact Ben Hescott, who can tell you more about the different ways of getting involved in research as an undergraduate, and can help direct you to different faculty members whose research might be a good fit. Interested in being my TA? That's great! All our TAs are selected using a central application process, so please submit and application and mark my course as one that you are interested in.
